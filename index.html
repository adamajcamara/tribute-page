Mufti Ismail Menk

Mufti Ismail Menk is a prominent Islamic scholar from Zimbabwe, widely recognized for his motivational talks and lectures. He focuses on spreading knowledge, peace, and guidance to Muslims all over the world.

Achievements

  • International Islamic lecturer and scholar
  • Named one of the 500 most influential Muslims in the world
  • Author of multiple Islamic books
  • Founder of the Mufti Menk Foundation

Famous Quote

"Do not be a slave to others when Allah has created you free."
